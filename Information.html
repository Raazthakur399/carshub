⚙️ How Cars Work

Cars move using energy from: Petrol (gasoline) engines Diesel engines Electric motors (battery-powered) Hybrid systems (fuel + electricity) The engine or motor turns the wheels, which makes the car move.

🧩 Main Parts of a Car

Engine/Motor – produces power Battery – provides electrical energy Fuel tank – stores petrol or diesel (not in electric cars) Wheels & Tires – help the car move and grip the road Brakes – slow down or stop the car Steering wheel – helps control direction

🛡️ Safety Features

Seat belts Airbags Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) Rear-view cameras and sensors

🌱 Types of Cars

Sedan – regular family car SUV – bigger and higher, good for rough roads Hatchback – small and compact Electric car – eco-friendly, no fuel Sports car – fast and stylish

🔧 Car Care

Regular oil changes Check tire pressure Keep brakes and lights working Wash and clean the car
